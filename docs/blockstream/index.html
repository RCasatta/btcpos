Blockstream Green

Export your CT descriptor from Blockstream Green wallet

Blockstream Green is a secure Bitcoin wallet developed by Blockstream, the company behind the Liquid Network. This tutorial shows you how to export your Liquid CT (Confidential Transaction) descriptor to use with Bitcoin POS for receiving private Liquid payments.

Security Note

The CT descriptor is a watch-only credential that allows you to generate receiving addresses and monitor your balance. It does not contain your private keys, so sharing it with Bitcoin POS is safe and will not give anyone the ability to spend your funds.
