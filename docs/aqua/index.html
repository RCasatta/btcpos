Aqua Wallet

Export your CT descriptor from Aqua wallet by JAN3

Aqua is a mobile Bitcoin wallet developed by JAN3 that supports both Bitcoin and the Liquid Network. This tutorial shows you how to export your Liquid CT (Confidential Transaction) descriptor, which allows you to receive payments on the Liquid Network using Bitcoin POS without exposing your private keys.

Security Note

The CT descriptor is a watch-only credential that allows you to generate receiving addresses and monitor your balance. It does not contain your private keys, so sharing it with Bitcoin POS is safe and will not give anyone the ability to spend your funds.
