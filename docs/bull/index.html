Bull Bitcoin

Export your CT descriptor from Bull Bitcoin wallet

Bull Bitcoin is a Canadian self-custodial Bitcoin wallet that supports the Liquid Network for instant, low-fee payments. This tutorial shows you how to export your Liquid CT (Confidential Transaction) descriptor to use with Bitcoin POS for receiving Liquid payments.

Security Note

The CT descriptor is a watch-only credential that allows you to generate receiving addresses and monitor your balance. It does not contain your private keys, so sharing it with Bitcoin POS is safe and will not give anyone the ability to spend your funds.
